Who is Lucas Dalto?

Lucas Dalto is an Argentinian programmer and content creator, known for his YouTube channel Soy Dalto, where he teaches programming and web development for free. His content is primarily aimed at beginners and is characterized by clear and direct explanations about technologies like HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Through his courses, he has helped thousands of people get started in the world of programming and improve their job opportunities.
